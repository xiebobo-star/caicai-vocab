### 场景对话

**场景：** 大学毕业后的职业选择 — Lily 面临人生十字路口。
**人物：** 小明 (Xiaoming), 莉莉 (Lily)

---

**Xiaoming:** Hey Lily, I heard you're thinking of changing your major. Is that true?

**Lily:** Yeah, I've been struggling. The engineering program is so intense. I need to **adapt** my study habits if I want to survive this semester.

**Xiaoming:** I understand. Moving from high school to university requires a huge **adjustment** — you really need time to **adjust** to the new pace.

**Lily:** Exactly! Sometimes I feel like **abandoning** everything and just switching to an easier subject. But I know that would be a waste of two years of hard work.

**Xiaoming:** Don't give up! The government's new tech policy will **accelerate** job growth in engineering. More opportunities are coming.

**Lily:** That's reassuring. But what about the **adverse** effects of staying? I'm worried my grades will suffer if the courses get even harder.

**Xiaoming:** Every challenge has its difficulties, but remember — you've already **adapted** to so many changes before. Give yourself time to **adjust**.

**Lily:** You're right. I won't **abandon** my goals just because things are tough. Maybe I just need to **accelerate** my learning pace and find better strategies.

**Xiaoming:** That's the spirit! Even under **adverse** conditions, you can thrive if you keep pushing forward.

#### 2. 填空
根据句意和括号中的中文提示，填入正确的单词（注意词形变化）：

a) After moving to a new country, it took her several months to __________ to the different culture. (适应)

b) The government introduced new policies to __________ economic growth. (加速)

c) The captain ordered the crew to __________ the ship because of the severe storm. (弃船)

d) Despite the __________ weather conditions, the rescue team continued searching. (不利的)

e) You should __________ your study habits to meet the new requirements. (调整)
