### 场景对话

**场景：** 周末聚会计划 — 看电影还是玩剧本杀？
**人物：** 小明 (Xiaoming), 莉莉 (Lily), 王老师 (Teacher Wang)

---

**Xiaoming:** Hey everyone, are you free this Saturday? Let's do something fun together!

**Lily:** Great idea! How many of us will **attend** the activity?

**Xiaoming:** I think about 10 people. The new cinema can **accommodate** all of us in one screening room.

**Lily:** Perfect! But first, let me **confirm** the movie schedule with the theater.

**Teacher Wang:** Wait — I heard there's a new 剧本杀 (murder mystery) cafe nearby! The scenario is about an ancient kingdom — someone tries to **assassinate** the emperor.

**Xiaoming:** Oh that sounds exciting! And the game rules **bind** everyone to play their roles without revealing secrets.

**Lily:** Hmm, now I'm torn. Movie is easy — we just **attend** and watch. But the murder mystery sounds more fun since we all work together.

**Xiaoming:** Can the venue **accommodate** 10 people for the murder mystery?

**Teacher Wang:** Yes, they have a large room that fits 12. I'll call to **confirm** the booking.

**Lily:** Great! Movie next weekend then. Let's **attend** the murder mystery this Saturday!
