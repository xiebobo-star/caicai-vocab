### 场景对话

**场景：** 大学社团的竞赛筹备 — Alex 和队友们正在准备全国机器人大赛
**人物：** Alex（队长）, Mei（技术组组长）, 指导老师 Dr. Chen

---

**Alex:** Alright team, the national robotics competition is in three months. Let's discuss what we want to **achieve** this year.

**Mei:** Last year we only made it to the semifinals. I think we can **accomplish** more if we improve our robot's navigation system.

**Dr. Chen:** That's a great point. I've seen you **accumulate** a lot of useful data from last year's failure — use that experience wisely.

**Alex:** Exactly! If we want to **acquire** the skills needed for a complex navigation algorithm, we should start practicing coding every weekend.

**Mei:** Agreed. But I'm worried about the other teams — some universities are using **aggression** tactics like jamming signals during matches.

**Alex:** Don't worry about their dirty tricks. If we focus on what we can control and **achieve** small milestones each week, we'll be ready.

**Dr. Chen:** A wise approach. Remember, the greatest **accomplishment** isn't just winning — it's the knowledge you **acquire** along the way.

**Mei:** True. And the experience we **accumulate** this year will help us even if we don't win the championship.

**Alex:** Let's set a goal: by the end of this month, we **accomplish** a working prototype. Then we **accumulate** more data and refine it.

**Mei:** Sounds like a plan! Let's **achieve** something amazing together! 💪

### 💡 易混词提醒

| 单词 | 区别 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| **achieve** vs **accomplish** | achieve 强调"达到目标/梦想"，accomplish 强调"完成任务/使命" | She **achieved** her dream. / They **accomplished** the mission. |
| **acquire** vs **obtain** | acquire 强调"通过努力习得"，obtain 更中性"获得" | **Acquire** knowledge vs **obtain** a ticket |
| **accumulate** vs **collect** | accumulate 强调"逐渐累积（大量）"，collect 更有目的性"收集" | Dust **accumulates**. / I **collect** stamps. |
| **aggression** vs **invasion** | aggression 范围更广（言语/行为攻击），invasion特指"入侵"领土 | Verbal **aggression** / military **invasion** |
